Intro

Hi there！I‘m Lingdong Kong.

I'm currently pursuing the B.Sc. degree in Intelligence Science & Technology with the School of Automation Science & Engineering, South China University of Technology (SCUT), Guangzhou, China.

I've been conducting research projects on neural network and system engineering with Prof. Zhijun Zhang at Human-Robot Intelligence Lab, Centre for Brain Computer Interfaces and Brain Information Processing, SCUT, since October 2016.

My research interests include automatic control, recurrent neural network, path planning and robotics.

Projects

＊05/2017 - 05/2018 “Solution and Applications of Recurrent Neural Networks for Solving Time-Varying Problems”
National Undergraduate Innovative and Entrepreneurial Training Program
   Purpose: This project aims to theoretically analyze the robustness of varying-parameter recurrent neural network, and to test the convergence performance of residual errors when perturbed by noises. Moreover, apply the neural model to robot tracking and venture investment problems.
   -Designed a novel power-type varying-parameter recurrent neural network and theoretically proved its super-exponential convergence rate based on Lyapunov stability theory.
   -Designed a motion planning scheme based on the proposed neural network for solving inverse kinematics of a Kinova JACO2 robot manipulator.
   -Wrote the MATLAB codes for solving robot tracking and venture investment problems.
   -Won the Outstanding Project Award (Top 10% in SCUT).
